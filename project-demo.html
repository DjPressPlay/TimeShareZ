Basics & Story

Each project is split into 100 ticket spots. Ticket price = Funding Goal ÷ 100. Example: $2,500 goal → $25 per ticket.

Run Length — Quick and secure, to support small startups and fresh ideas.
Funding Goal — Max $5,000, capped to short durations to stay within regulations.
Ticket Spots — Tickets represent claim entries for the rewards listed. Users buy access, not guaranteed items.
Spots — Each ticket represents a spot in the auction pool. Rewards are based on when users join: early = better gifts, late = more cash back.

All funds are held in USDC stablecoin until the run ends. If the goal isn't met by the deadline, everyone gets refunded automatically.
